Class 1 – Understanding AI Products
Key Learnings
AI products are not only about technology but about solving business and customer problems.
The focus of this course is practical application of AI.
Product managers should think like CEOs.
AI should create societal and customer value.
Definitions are less important than understanding use cases and business impact.
My Understanding of AI Products

An AI product is any product that uses AI capabilities such as prediction, recommendation, automation, natural language understanding, or intelligent decision-making to improve customer experience or business outcomes.
